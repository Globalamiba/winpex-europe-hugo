Winpex Europe GmbH: A Part of the Winpex Group Legacy


Winpex opened for business in 2003, with a registered fund of 100 million RMB, and has grown into a dynamic and trusted supplier of high-quality products and services to customers worldwide. Over the years, our dedication to excellence and innovation has allowed us to expand our capabilities and offerings.


Winpex Group's Journey


In the past two decades, Winpex Group has continually evolved and expanded its presence in the global market. With the diligent efforts of our colleagues, our business has experienced constant and stable growth. Today, Winpex Group comprises 12 subsidiaries, 3 overseas offices, 3 solely owned factories, and a team of more than 800 dedicated staff members. We have achieved an annual sales volume in excess of US$370 million. Our multifaceted approach allows us to serve as more than just a ""General Merchandise Supply Chain Management"" expert. We've diversified our commercial functions to include ""Self-support Export,"" ""Foreign Trade Development,"" ""Multi-Products Manufacturing,"" and ""Cross-border Electronic Commerce."" Through the tireless work of our colleagues, we've rapidly expanded our product range to encompass over 70,000 products across 18 categories, from ""Household"" and ""Outdoor Solar Lights"" to ""Kitchen and Bathroom"" and ""Crafts and Gifts."" We've cultivated a supply chain of over 3,000 highly cooperative domestic manufacturers to provide quality products to our network of over 1,000 strategic customers. Our customer base now spans more than 60 countries, including the EU countries, UK, USA, Canada, Australia, Japan, Russia, and South America. To meet the demands of our growing customer base, we've established two state-of-the-art factories focused on producing ""green"" products, such as solar-powered lights and eco-friendly household items. Winpex has been widely praised for our commitment to environmental protection while expanding our business. We remain dedicated to becoming a leading supplier in the International Trading Business, providing the highest quality products and services to our global customers. Our aim is to continue growing and improving together with our valued customers through mutual support.
